Pinzolo & Trentino Travel Guide

📍 Area Overview

Pinzolo is a charming town in the Trentino-Alto Adige region, surrounded by the majestic Dolomites. This region is known for its rich history, alpine traditions, and a mix of Italian and Austrian influences. The area offers a blend of outdoor adventures, local food, history, and cultural experiences.

🏰 Historical & Cultural Sites

Church of San Vigilio

A 10th-century church famous for its Danse Macabre frescoes painted in 1539.
